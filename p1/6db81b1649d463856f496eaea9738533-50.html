According to Koontz and O’Donnell

Staffing is defined as,

•

“Filling and keeping filled, positions in the organizational structure.”

•

This is done by

•

identifying work-force requirements ,

•

inventorying the people available,

•

Recruiting & selecting

•

Placing & promotion,

•

Appraising & planning the careers,

•

compensating,

•

Training & developing existing staff or new recruits,

so that they can accomplish their tasks effectively and efficiently
